Minas Gerais is a landlocked state in Brazil that often eludes tourist itineraries. This is a pity as it has lush rolling hills, is Brazil’s center for homemade comfort food, produces the best cheese and cachaça, has proper winters, and is home to endearing towns and cities. Here are some of the most beautiful towns in Minas Gerais.
